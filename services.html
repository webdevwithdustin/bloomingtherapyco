Services

Initial welcoming session

Session length: 60-75 minutes

Together, we will assess your well-being needs and preferences, and design a unique therapy program, just for you.

We will take our time reviewing consent, confidentiality, and our policies for an informed therapy experience.

Blooming sessions

Session length: 60-75 minutes

In home, 1:1 sessions, uniquely tailored to your well-being goals.

Our therapists provide structure to sessions, ensuring that you reap the benefits of mindful aging, creative expression, storytelling, and new experiences. You will do the rest!

Connection sessions

Session length: 20-30 minutes (virtual only)

With your consent, your therapist can connect with the important people in your life (such as family, friends, spiritual mentors, and medical providers) to support you with your well-being goals.
